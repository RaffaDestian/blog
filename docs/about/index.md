# About This Blog

## Mission

This blog documents my personal journey navigating the intersection of traditional software development and the emerging AI-powered coding paradigm. My goal is to share practical insights, lessons learned, and observations about how AI is reshaping our craft.

## Why "Vibe Coding"?

The term "vibe coding" captures the essence of modern AI-assisted development:

- **Flow State**: AI tools help maintain momentum and creative flow
- **Intuitive**: Less syntax wrestling, more problem-solving
- **Collaborative**: Working with AI as a pair programming partner
- **Iterative**: Quick experiments and rapid prototyping
- **Enjoyable**: Making coding more fun and less tedious
